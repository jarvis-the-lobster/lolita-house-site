What would you like to discuss?

A PDF copy of these answers is created in your browser and emailed to Lolita House.

  1. Reach out Call, email, or send a simple inquiry about current availability.
  2. Screening Gather initial contact details and basic background information to initiate the screening.
  3. Interview (either phone or in person) Evaluate their needs and situation to determine if they are a good fit for our community and if we can properly support them.
  4. Financial Confirmation Verify and confirm the applicant's finances, funding sources, or agency administrative coverage to ensure programmatic and rental fees are sustainable.
  5. Agreement and rules Sign Licensed Agreement. Lolita House uses License Agreement (not Lease agreement) to intake all residents. At this point, we go over house rules, community expectation and schedules in detail to ensure complete alignment. All residents MUST have a signed License agreement before move-in. No exceptions.
  6. Move-in Welcome to Lolita House, a place to thrive.
